Media Publicity

Publicity is the movement of information to the general public from the media. No promotional tool is more powerful than publicity. To generate publicity with social media, interaction and engagement with individual, groups, people, crowd, congress or a mere fan, is the key. The role of publicists has always been to promote the public image and reputation of people, products and businesses. It is their job to develop a strategy, shape a consistent message, work with the media, and create exposure. From a marketing perspective, publicity is one component of promotion and marketing. The other elements of the promotional mix are advertising, sales promotion, direct marketing and personal selling. We help clients look good by arranging their publicity through various mediums and across different digital platforms. Once we develop the company’s message which we call Media-grade content, our PR gets to work. This experienced team actively pitches your story to the desired media outlets. And, we’re not talking about emailing a press release and hoping for the best. Our publicists have access to Media Relations Agencies, web news portals, press, and other means of digital media platforms. They know who to talk to, where to post, when to post, sanitize comments, develop a story and what story will appeal to whom, what story could be special and what it takes to build up the brand value. Whatever our team does it very well.

Media publicity

The question has been raised lately about the value of publicity in today’s market. Of course, we accept we are biased, but we think you need publicity more than ever, and here are a few reasons why: A. Social Media Social media tools allows us to help you interlock all the activities into a cohesive campaign. Through Twitter, Facebook, LinkedIn, YouTube, Slideshare, features on websites, and blog reviews you can get more exposure and help us build the clients reputations in their target communities. B. Timing Social media has also changed the timing of marketing campaigns. Blogging and social media are only tools. We map out a campaign strategy so you know what to blog and tweet about. Instead of creating a blitz campaign of shock and awe (advertising driven), today we need a sustained campaign of community building and long-term exposure. The strategy we design is blended carefully into the short- and long-term goals of the client. To check out timing: Google Trends can give you an idea about when your topic becomes a tipping point on digital platform. C. Cost-Effective As amazing as your product or company may be, it needs help to find its market niche and standing. It is more cost-effective to tailor the relationship-building campaign to its niche markets through blogger outreach, Web publicity and social media interactions than to spend the money on advertising in mass media. D. Access Although getting access has become easier, gaining attention is tougher than ever. Access to journalists and producers are no longer the domain of the well-connected publicist, but again those relationships are more important than ever. Businesses are getting pitched through email, not to mention on their blogs, Twitter feeds, LinkedIn and even on Facebook. We build your relationship with editors, journalists, producers, freelancers, and bloggers over months and years. This keeps you on the surface and tops your company’s name up from time to time. E. Reputation Monitoring Word of mouth and positive reputation is not only important today, it is the essential growth element for all persons and businesses. Monitoring reputation has come a long way. Creating good will and a good customer experience is important for the tech savvy consumer who is a click away from switching to another similar solution provider or an expert. With search tools like Google, Topsy, and Social Mention, we stay current with the conversations, and react more quickly to protect our client’s reputations as well as to take advantage of any hot-button issues. We involve different and various sources to create presence online where your business is noticed on social media circuit. Digital presence is increased which is the main domain of online marketing awareness to connect you to your clients far and wide. Over the past year, our team has arranged thousands of posts online, radio coverage, and acres of print and digital coverage. We are that solution provider which has accomplished more. What makes us so successful? I think it’s because our team is honestly focused on helping our clients look their best. We can’t help it. We want you to succeed.
