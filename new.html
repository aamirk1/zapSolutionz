Operations involve:


1. Using and managing assets, equipment, and systems to achieve desired outcomes

2. Monitoring and controlling performance

3. Optimizing output and productivity

4. Ensuring safety and compliance


Maintenance involves:


1. Performing routine and preventive tasks to prevent failures

2. Repairing and replacing parts or equipment as needed

3. Conducting inspections and diagnostics

4. Upgrading or modifying equipment to improve performance


O&M activities can be divided into several categories, including:


1. Preventive Maintenance (PM)

2. Corrective Maintenance (CM)

3. Predictive Maintenance (PdM)

4. Reliability-Centered Maintenance (RCM)


Effective O&M practices help organizations:


1. Reduce downtime and increase availability

2. Improve efficiency and productivity

3. Extend asset lifespan

4. Enhance safety and compliance

5. Optimize costs and resource allocation


O&M is crucial in energy, transportation & facilities management.
